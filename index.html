Magoro

As a mobile computing final project we were proposed to carry out an android mobile application. Our application is called Magoro.

Magoro is a fitness application with an entertainment aspect whose goal is to take care of virtual pets, little monsters, the Magoros, who need the help of users to be fed and feel happy.

Magoro's happiness is reflected by the amount of physical exercise practiced, be this running, walking, or any activity that involves the user's movement, while the food is reflected by the ingredients, that are purchased with coins obtained in various ways (exercise, games, sleep awards, achievements).

Being the application focused on the fitness aspect, when the physical activity is finished, there is a report that is shown to the user, the route made, as well as the detailed data of the race. The other fitness part of the application is the information that is provided when you wake the Magoros up regarding the quality of the user's sleep.
